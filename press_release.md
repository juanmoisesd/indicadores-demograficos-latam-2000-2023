### New Open Dataset Published: Derived demographic indicators for Latin America (2000-2023). CC0 1.0

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Derived demographic indicators for Latin America (2000-2023). CC0 1.0*.

The dataset is freely available under the **CC0 1.0 Universal** license, allowing unrestricted use, distribution, and reproduction.
